Indie Map is a social graph for the IndieWeb, with the data to back it up.

Indie Map is...

  • An interactive map of the IndieWeb social graph.
  • An API to access that social graph.
  • A SQL queryable dataset of >4.5M HTML pages (>TODO GB) and parsed microformats from the >1500 most active IndieWeb sites.
  • A set of interesting statistics, charts, and graphs.
  • A complete mirror of all HTML pages on those sites, in WARC format.

Indie Map is free, open source, and placed into the public domain. Crawled content remains the property of individual sites, under their existing copyright.
